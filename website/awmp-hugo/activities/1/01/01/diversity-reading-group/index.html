Diversity reading group

A reading group about research on gender gap, participation of women and minorities in academia and the job market. If you want to join, contact us!

Diversity and gender equality are subtle issues and we need to base our understanding and actions on solid grounds. That is why we are interested in scientific research about gender gap, participation of women and minorities in academia and the job market.

We meet every month and present in turns an article or a book. If you are interested in joining us, please contact Chiara Boccato at chiaraDOTboccatoATunimiDOTit.

If you would like to receive updates and information about the reading group presentations (which are held by zoom) please tick the corresponding box in the registration form “AWMP activities - registration to mailing lists and working groups” !

Here is what we red so far:

  • “Frauen in der Mathematik. Thomas Vogt im Gespräch mit Marie-Françoise Roy” by Thomas Vogt (link). The article presents the project Gender gap in science.

  • “An Inclusive Academy” by A. Stewart and V. Valian

  • “An implicit gender sex-science association in the general population and STEM faculty” by M. Marini and M. R. Banaji. (link)

  • “What works: gender equality by design” by I. Bohnet.
